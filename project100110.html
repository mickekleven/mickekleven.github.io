The story

This was an interesting project where I was given overall responsibility for developing the site from scratch. I didn't have much experience in web development at the time and saw this as a great opportunity to acquire the skills I lacked in web programming. I found it both fun and educational. I managed to stick to the schedule even though there was a lot of other stuff on my table that I wasn't really used to during this projekt.

Overall I'm satisfied with what i accomplished back then but today I whould have done everything, differently. But off course, now I have these skills I lacked before and that makes a huge different, obviously.
